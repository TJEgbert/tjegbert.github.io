Work History

My past work and current work history

Mowing Lawns 2003 to 2009

My brother and I would mow lawns for a handful of people around our neighborhood. Once my brother got his license we branched out and took a couple more lawns that were outside walking distance.

Target 2012 to 2019

I was hired as a cashier but within my first ninety days I had already been transferred over to the Electronics department. A couple years in when one of my coworkers was promoted, I took over the position of setting the new release movies, books, and CDs. My last two years I was there I was the unofficial manager over the electronics. My manager would leave me in charge of the Electronics department while she managed her other areas.

McKenzie Taxidermy Supply 2019 to 2023

I was hired as a warehouse worker pulling orders and shipping them out. With my willingness to get the job done I was trained in every position at the warehouse. I also got promoted to assistant manager after the previous assistant manager got promoted to manager of the warehouse. This led to me running the team and making sure everything got done.
