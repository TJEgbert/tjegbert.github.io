About Me

My name is Trevor Egbert I was born on July 16 1993 I am the youngest of three kids. I am the proud uncle of four necies and newphews with another right around the conner Growing up like most kids what I wanted to be change drastically. Astronaut, forensic technician and architect to name a few. There was always one thing I went back to though and that was working on video games. Properly since playing video games were and still is my favorite past time. Once I hit High School my goal was to make video games. With that as a goal I went to the Davis Technical College to get a certification in animation. My animation skills where good but my 3D modeling skills not so much. I ended completing the course, but I never did anything with it because of my 3D modeling skills. Cut to 2020 I ended up getting a course on Udemy on Unreal Engine with C++. I found with doing that course I had a knack for coding. With rising prices and a goal in mind I applied to Weber State University in 2021 to hope of getting into the video game industry. If for some reason that doesn't happen, I will at least have a fall back with a Computer Science degree.
